Vikasana Systems was founded on a single conviction: India's defense forces deserve a command architecture built entirely for them — no dependency, no compromise, no outsourcing.

OUR MISSION

One unified command for every unmanned system

Military forces deploying drones, UAVs, UGVs, and RCWS platforms face a critical vulnerability: siloed ground control stations, incompatible protocols, and vendor lock-in leave commanders blind when seconds matter most.

We are closing that gap. Vikasana Systems delivers India's most advanced military UXV command software — purpose-built for army, navy, and air force operations. Our platform is designed to scale with the mission, from a single surveillance UAV to a coordinated multi-vehicle autonomous strike package.

From protocol stacks to AI algorithms — we own and control the entire command stack. No outsourcing. No vendor lock-in. No gaps.

01

Full Vertical Ownership

Protocol stacks, AI inference engines, datalink security, and user interface — all built and maintained in-house. No third-party dependency in the mission-critical path.

02

Defense-Grade Reliability

Every component validated for -30°C to +50°C operation, 95% humidity, dust, shock, and EMI environments. Software that performs under the harshest conditions on earth.

03

Continuous AI Advancement

Threat detection models updated with new drone signatures, vehicle patterns, and behavioral profiles. The platform becomes more capable with every mission cycle.

04

Made in India

Designed, developed, and deployed from India — aligned with Atmanirbhar Bharat and the nation's defense modernization goals. Built to export, built to trust.
